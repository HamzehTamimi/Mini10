Add here your custom registry (.reg) files.
They will be automatically merged into the image registry.

Usage:
- HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\MINI10_SOFTWARE for HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\SOFTWARE
- HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\MINI10_SYSTEM for HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\SYSTEM
- HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\MINI10_NTUSER for HKEY_CURRENT_USER
- HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\MINI10_SOFTWARE_BOOT for boot.wim HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\SOFTWARE
- HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\MINI10_SYSTEM_BOOT for boot.wim HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\SYSTEM
